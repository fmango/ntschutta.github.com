--- layout: post meta: {} status: publish tags: - Apple title: 1 Million Videos...and Counting type: post published: true --- It always happens doesn't it? You buy some new-fangled gadget and a week later out comes a cheaper, faster, bigger, prettier gadget. Sigh. Back in late August I broke down and bought an iPod - mostly for our trip to Spain - and now we have video iPods. When I first heard rumors about watching a movie or TV show on an iPod, I had to admit I was skeptical. Well, I guess I should know better than to do doubt - Apple has sold more than a million videos in less than 20 days despite a rather limited content. I'm very interested to see how this experiment works especially as more shows and movies are added. While I doubt I'll be buying one soon (maybe I can give "my" iPod to Christine and buy a newer, shinier video iPod...yeah, I doubt it) I have to admit, I would have watched an episode of Lost in Spain...
