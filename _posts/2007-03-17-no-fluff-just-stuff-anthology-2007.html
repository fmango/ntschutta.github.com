For those of you who have (inexplicably) ignored my advice, you can get a flavor of what a typical symposium is like by reading the latest and greatest No Fluff Just Stuff Anthology, Volume II. But don't just take my word for it, see what Neal "let's-see-how-many-books-I-can-write-in-one-year" Ford (otherwise known as the cat wrangler of the anthology) has to say here. You can order your very own copy from the Prags or Amazon (don't forget, Anthologies make *great* gifts). Enjoy!
