--- layout: post meta: {} status: publish tags: - Development - Software title: What Can You do Without? type: post published: true --- I've touched on this before, but the next time someone says "what if a customer wants to do..." reply it just doesn't matter. The hardest part of software isn't figuring out what the system has to do - it's determining what you can do without. When all else fails, keep it simple already (via Presentation Zen).
